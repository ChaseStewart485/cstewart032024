Empathy and Innovation Define HP Amplify 2025

Rob Enderle

The article highlights HP’s unique approach to its partner relationships, particularly at its Amplify event, where CEO Enrique Lores personally engaged with attendees and partners, something rarely seen from other CEOs. The event focused on customer and partner needs, featuring HP's use of AI to match products with users and improve service. HP’s advancements in security, such as Quantum Safe printers, and its commitment to sustainability and product reuse were also key topics. The company is also expanding its AI PC product line and ensuring its supply chain is prepared for disruptions.

HP’s dedication to employee well-being and its strategic use of AI to train partners were also emphasized. The company’s ethical leadership, embodied by Lores, was recognized with a Lifetime Achievement Award. HP’s focus on innovation, customer care, and responsible business practices makes it stand out in the tech industry.
